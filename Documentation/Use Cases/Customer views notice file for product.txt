1. Title: Customer views notice file for product.
2. Primary Actor: Customer
3. Goal in Context: To let the customer know about packages and software used in different devices.
4. Stakeholders and Interests:
	1. Customer:
		1. To know about the information contained in notice file.
	2. Product Manager:
		1. To let customer know about packages used in their product as required by licenses.
		2. To distribute notice files to all customers upon any software updates.
5. Preconditions:
	1. The customer knows what a QR code is.
	2. The customer is able to scan a QR code.
	3. The customer has internet access.
6. Main Success Scenario:
	1. The customer is able to see the notice file online.
7. Failed End Condition:
	1. The website is down.
	2. The data base cannot be accessed.
8. Trigger: Customer receives message about software update.
9. Notes: There may be some cases where a direct URL would be preferable over a QR code.
